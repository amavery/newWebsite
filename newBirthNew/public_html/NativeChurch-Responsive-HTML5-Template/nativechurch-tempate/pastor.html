About Us

Pastor Brent

Pastor Brent Murphy is the founder and pastor of New Birth Bible Fellowship Church based in Raleigh, NC. He holds a Bachelors of Arts in History and Philosophy from the illustrious North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State University.

He is currently studying to receive his master of divinity from Duke University Divinity School, Durham, NC. Pastor Brent is Saved, Sanctified, and Holy Ghost Filled, and licensed through the United Holy Church of America, Inc.

About our Church

New Birth is a new church in the Raleigh area. Our mission is to Save Souls, Change Lives, and Transform Communities.

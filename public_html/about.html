Mission & Vision Statement

The Mission of New Birth Bible Fellowship is to Save Souls, Change Lives, and Transform Communities

The Vision of New Birth Bible Fellowship is to develop a multi-ethnic body of disciples, saved, sanctified, and spirit filled, prepared to execute the great commission through word and deed


Statement of Faith

  1. We believe the Holy Scriptures of the Old and New Testament to be the inspired, inerrant, and infallible Word of God.
  2. We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons; God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.
  3. We believe the only means to be saved from sin is through faith in Jesus Christ; His death, burial, and resurrection.
  4. We believe in a distinct moment of sanctification that commences an ongoing process of yielding to God's Word and His Spirit in order to complete development of Christ's character in us.
  5. We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as described in the Bible; it is the promise of the Father, to empower the life of the believer and the church. We believe the utterances of tongues to be the nominal occurrence of spirit baptism.
  6. We believe in the blessed Hope, which is the rapture of the Church of God, which is in Christ, at His return.
